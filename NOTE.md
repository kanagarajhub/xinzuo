## What I picked
Rebuilding and enhancing the broken Bundle Builder page 

## Why it's the highest-impact thing here
Bundle builders are one of the most highly requested features for Shopify e-commerce sites because they directly drive Average Order Value (AOV) and conversion rates. It allows customers to easily configure product sets and automatically unlock tiered discounts at checkout.

## What I did
1. Interactive Summary Dock: Redesigned the rigid bottom bar into a premium, centered floating glassmorphic dock. Made the thumbnails interactive so users can deselect items on click, complete with red delete badges on hover.
2. Native Quick View Modal: Added a clean HTML5 `<dialog>` modal to let users view detailed product descriptions and toggles without leaving the builder. Styled it with zoom micro-animations and smooth overlay backdrops.
3. Card Polish & Selected State: Shifted sale badges to avoid overlaps, added a golden border glow for selected items, and styled responsive layouts.
